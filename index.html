Mohalla Assi Stay

About us

Welcome to Mohalla Assi Stay, your one-stop shop for all things travel and relaxation in Varanasi.

Located in the heart of this ancient city, we offer a wide range of services to help you make the most of your trip.

Our boating services allow you to explore the beauty of the Ganges River and take in the stunning views of Varanasi's ghats and temples. Whether you're looking for a leisurely cruise or a more thrilling experience on a speedboat, we have something for everyone.

In addition to boating, we also offer tour and travel services to help you see all that Varanasi has to offer. From guided tours of the city's top landmarks to customized itineraries, we can help you plan the perfect vacation.

Need to exchange currency or need transportation to your next destination? We've got you covered with our money exchange and transportation services. And when it's time to relax and unwind, our yoga and meditation classes are the perfect way to find inner peace and balance.

With all of these services at your disposal, you can trust Mohalla Assi Stay to make your trip to Varanasi an enjoyable and stress-free experience. Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to serving you during your stay.
